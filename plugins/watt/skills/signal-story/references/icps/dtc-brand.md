# Direct-to-consumer brand

- **id:** dtc-brand
- **label:** Direct-to-consumer brand
- **reader:** A founder or growth lead at a DTC brand buying their own media. They
  own the number.
- **jtbd:** Show them who their real high-intent customer is and where to spend to
  grow acquisition efficiently.
- **kpis:** CAC, blended ROAS, new-customer growth, contribution margin, payback
  period.
- **vocabulary:**
  - *use:* "your customer", "acquisition", "spend", "channel mix", "growth",
    "payback".
  - *avoid:* agency/roster framing ("your client", "the pitch") — they are the end
    buyer.
- **objection:** "We know our customer." Preempt it with the underserved segment
  and the where-to-spend map — the part of the market they are not yet reaching.
- **cta:** Reallocate spend toward the under-tapped segment and geographies; pilot
  in one market before scaling the full audience.
- **lead_with:** the core high-intent audience and the where-to-spend map.
- **serves:** (none — the brand is the end reader)
- **evidence_hook:** cross the audience against the brand's own category to size the
  in-market pool they are not yet reaching. Run only when you want a hard number.
- **example_headline:** "The customer you're underspending on"
