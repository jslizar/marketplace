# Gated playbook drop

- **Archetype**: "I finally created the {superlative asset}" + proof stack + comment-to-receive gate
- **Funnel stage**: MOFU — makes the approach credible and captures evaluator intent via the comment gate.
- **Stage mechanic**: The asset promises the ICP's desired outcome; the proof stack (results, audience served) makes it credible; the comment gate converts interest into a DM-able list. Comment counts (7,919 on the top example) also feed reach.
- **Platform**: LinkedIn
- **Source examples**: curated from Millie's bank; refreshed only when the bank updates.
  - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ericlay-virio_after-3-years-of-testing-ive-finally-created-activity-7306317167380054017-QrGc — Eric Lay (Virio), 3,238 reactions / 7,919 comments, 2025-03-14
  - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/retentionadam_i-just-spoke-to-a-group-of-130-ceos-cmos-activity-7396968758415851520-8IUv — Adam Robinson (RB2B), 1,350 reactions / 1,499 comments, 2025-12-03
  - Candidate: Dylan Power "NEVER use GPT for your outreach again" (226 r / 1,521 c) — urgency-variant with 24h scarcity
- **Hook pattern**: "After {n} years of testing, I've finally created the most effective {asset} ever." / "I just spoke to {impressive audience} about {contrarian approach}... and I'm sharing the full playbook 👇"
- **Skeleton** (block sequence):
  1. Superlative-asset hook (time invested + the artifact)
  2. Skeptic's proof stack: "If you still don't trust it" + arrow-bulleted results with numbers
  3. What's inside: numbered contents of the asset (4–6 items)
  4. (Robinson variant) the intrigue story: what happened when the approach was tested
  5. The gate: "connect + comment '{keyword}' and I'll send it" — soft CTA, MOFU-legal
- **Rhythm & formatting**: Arrow bullets for proof, numbered list for contents, keyword in quotes. 150–350 words.
- **Why it works**: Reciprocity (free asset) + specificity (146 meetings, 60 days) + the gate manufactures a compliance action that doubles as intent data. The keyword comment is frictionless.
- **Adaptation notes**: Must change: the asset (must exist and be genuinely good), all proof numbers, the keyword. Must NOT change: proof-before-gate ordering — gating an unproven asset reads as a funnel trick.
- **Watch-outs**: The client must actually DM every commenter (automation or discipline). Overclaiming ("replaces a $80k/year team") invites hostile comments; keep superlatives inside what the Proof section supports.
