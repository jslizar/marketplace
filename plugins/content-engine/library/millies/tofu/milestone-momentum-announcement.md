# Milestone momentum announcement

- **Archetype**: raise/quarter/ARR announcement structured as gratitude + momentum receipts, closing on a movement line
- **Funnel stage**: TOFU — earns reach on the back of company news; the celebration invites the whole ecosystem to tag itself in.
- **Stage mechanic**: The milestone is the news peg; the structure credits everyone (users, team, investors) so each named group amplifies. The final line universalizes ("This is the age of the builder") to give non-stakeholders a reason to share.
- **Platform**: LinkedIn
- **Source examples**: curated from Millie's bank; refreshed only when the bank updates.

  - Candidate (from Millie's bank): https://www.linkedin.com/posts/carlesreina_q2-was-unique-at-elevenlabs-we-had-share-7480216645743972352-uOvi — Carles Reina (ElevenLabs) quarterly-roundup variant, 445 reactions, 2026-07-07
- **Hook pattern**: "{Company} just raised ${n} at a ${n} valuation." / "We just raised another ${n} after {growth stat}. The crazy part is this almost never happened."
- **Skeleton** (block sequence):
  1. The milestone, stated flat with the numbers
  2. (Optional origin detour — Tanay variant: the 17-years-ago story that makes the milestone a payoff)
  3. Gratitude ledger: users → team → investors, each with a concrete stat or name
  4. Proof-of-momentum block (retention, Fortune 500 count, visits — 3–5 stats)
  5. Movement close: one line that makes it about the era, not the company — no CTA
- **Rhythm & formatting**: Dense with named entities and numbers; list-like gratitude block; 200–450 words. Bullet dashes for stats.
- **Why it works**: Borrowed celebration — every named investor, customer, and teammate is a distribution node. The origin-story variant adds the underdog arc that makes strangers care.
- **Adaptation notes**: Must change: all numbers and names (Canon-verifiable only). Must NOT change: gratitude-before-stats ordering and the universalizing close.
- **Watch-outs**: Without real momentum numbers it's just a press release. Don't thank abstractions ("our amazing community") — name things.
