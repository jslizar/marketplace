# Open feedback bounty

- **Archetype**: insider asks the audience for unfiltered criticism of their own platform/product, with a bounty for the best answer
- **Funnel stage**: TOFU — earns reach through comment volume; the ask IS the content.
- **Stage mechanic**: Inverts the usual flow — instead of broadcasting, the insider invites the pile-on everyone's been saving up. Comment counts explode (960 comments on 1,627 reactions), which the algorithm rewards. The bounty legitimizes spicy answers.
- **Platform**: LinkedIn
- **Source examples**: curated from Millie's bank; refreshed only when the bank updates.
  - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/sugju-choi_hey-linkedin-im-linkedins-one-of-the-first-activity-7419070510862319616-tSUL — Sugi Choi (Virio; the post role-plays a LinkedIn insider — the bank titles it "Joined LinkedIn"), 1,627 reactions / 960 comments, 2026-01-19
  - (Single-example spec — pin a second example when one appears in Millie's bank.)
- **Hook pattern**: "Hey {audience}! I'm {insider credential} — and I want your UNFILTERED feedback. What do you HATE the most about {thing}?"
- **Skeleton** (block sequence):
  1. Direct-address hook with insider credential + the blunt question
  2. Persona roll-call ("whether you're a: recruiter / founder / job seeker / creator...") — everyone finds their line
  3. Permission beat ("doesn't matter why you're here — I wanna know what frustrates you")
  4. Commitment beat ("I'll read every single one - even the spicy ones")
  5. Bounty close: a concrete prize for the best answer
- **Rhythm & formatting**: Conversational, caps for emphasis (UNFILTERED, HATE), angle-bracket persona list, 100–200 words. Short by design.
- **Why it works**: Asymmetry (insider asking to be roasted) + guaranteed audience relevance via the persona roll-call + a bounty that converts lurkers into commenters. Comments are the product.
- **Adaptation notes**: Must change: the credential (must be real standing over the thing being critiqued) and the bounty. Must NOT change: the persona roll-call and the read-every-one commitment — they drive the comment volume.
- **Watch-outs**: Only works if the client genuinely has insider standing AND will engage the comments for days. An unanswered pile-on is brand damage. Bounty must actually be delivered.

## Top example — full post text

Hey LinkedIn! I'm LinkedIn's one of the first ever Product Builder - and I want your UNFILTERED feedback.

What do you HATE the most about LinkedIn?

Whether you're a:
> recruiter or a founder looking for the right talent
> job seeker hunting for opportunities
> creator building your personal brand
> developer sharing your work
> or just here to network

Doesn't matter WHY you're here, but I wanna know WHAT frustrates you the most.

Leave a comment below, I'll read every single one - even the spicy ones.

Bonus - I will give 6 month of FREE LinkedIn premium ($180 worth) to the best suggestion! 💗
