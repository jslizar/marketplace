# Escalation timeline

- **Archetype**: Stat hook + year-by-year escalation list + zoom-out implication
- **Funnel stage**: TOFU — earns reach with the ICP; client-agnostic on the surface.
- **Stage mechanic**: The timeline makes a trend FEEL exponential instead of just claiming it — readers share the "whoa" of watching the numbers climb. Positions the author as someone tracking the future.
- **Platform**: LinkedIn
- **Source examples**: exactly 2, refreshed by the scheduled library-refresh task from the viral_posts_all corpus.

  - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/harbech_amazon-just-revealed-they-now-have-more-than-activity-7121124632673300480-OfsQ — Martin Harbech (Meta), 10,036 reactions / 462 comments, 2023-10-20
- **Hook pattern**: "{Company/industry} has {jaw-drop current number} {things} deployed to {goal}."
- **Skeleton** (block sequence):
  1. Jaw-drop stat hook (the endpoint of the timeline)
  2. One-line promise: "Here's a snapshot of the journey"
  3. Then-vs-now contrast (a decade ago it seemed impossible / today it's live)
  4. The timeline: dash-list, one year per line, number climbing
  5. Highlight line pulling out the steepest jump ("the last two years alone…")
  6. Zoom-out: what the trend means for work/industry
  7. Closing challenge or open question about what's next
- **Rhythm & formatting**: 200–300 words. Dash-list timeline is the centerpiece. Numbers formatted consistently. Hashtags optional; no CTA.
- **Why it works**: A sequence of rising numbers is self-evidently credible in a way an adjective never is. The reader does the extrapolation themselves, which makes the conclusion feel like their own.
- **Adaptation notes**: The timeline must be real, sourced, and check out (readers WILL verify the endpoint stat). Pick the trend the ICP is professionally anxious about. The steepest-jump highlight line is the share trigger — never cut it.
- **Watch-outs**: A linear trend dressed up as exponential gets ratio'd. Ending on doom instead of implication caps reach — end on "what this means", not "we're all doomed".
