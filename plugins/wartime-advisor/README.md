# wartime-advisor

A strategic work advisor for leadership calls under pressure. Ask it anything shaped like "what should I do about X" — team performance, churn, hiring/firing, growth under stress — and it diagnoses the situation through two frameworks before advising:

- **Peacetime / Wartime CEO** (Ben Horowitz) — which mode the situation demands, and what that changes about the right move
- **Strategic Inflection Points** (Andy Grove, *Only the Paranoid Survive*) — whether the ground is shifting 10x under you
- Plus a **skill × culture-fit 2x2** for people decisions

It's direct, tells you when your framing is wrong, and is post-sales aware. No setup, no MCP dependencies.

The frameworks are credited to their authors' published work; the skill applies them, it doesn't reproduce the books.
